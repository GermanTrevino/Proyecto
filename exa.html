Primera Parte

What is iterative design? Explain its importance in interaction design.

Iterative design is an approach of incrementally developing and refining a design based on feedback and evaluation. Iterative design can apply to a learning experience, the creation of media, or the development of learning systems.

It is important in interactive design because interactive design is mainly based on the user interaction and user experience. Given that iterative design uses as its main tool the user feedback, it helps directly to correct or improve the design of a page or application regarding user experience directly.

Explain, in your project, how do you achieve the following: action, knowledge, passion.

Action

There is a lot of action in this project and it can be seen in the whole process of operation. First of all we are always acting because the application must be running 24/7. Why? Because hospitals never close they are always open giving medicare to everyone that needs it. Second, it is not only to keep the application running it is also been monitoring the service that the application is giving and also take on account the feedback since a little mistake on the service can cost a human life and that is our main goal: keep saving lives.

Knowledge

Knowledge is achieved almost instantaneous due to the fact that in order to successfully finish our project it is necessary to do a lot of research such as: How do the hospital operate (general and particularly), type of stretchers, and of course the different type of emergencies.

Passion

This project is full of passion, there is no other way to describe it because what we are looking for is to save human lives, no more. We just want to offer the community a better healthcare attention by supplying them a better stretcher request mechanism that can potentially save somebody in an extreme emergency.

Create your own methodology to develop a user interface. It’s important to justify all the steps.

  1. Brainstorm

    A brainstorm is crucial as the first step because you can come up with a lot of ideas and then choose the one that suits better.

  2. Draw it in a piece of paper

    Then you may want to develop the idea on a piece of paper so everyone can visualize it and contribute to the idea in an adequate way.

  3. Sketch it on (balsamiq, proto.io, photoshop, etc.)

    After the whole drawing process is done it is important to jump to a software that can simulate this interface digitally.

  4. Start implementing the interface on the html/app

    Once the idea is very clear and it’s sketch on paper and digitally its time to star implementing the idea. It would be a lot easier to do because the whole idea its very clear and there is also a “sample” of the design.

  5. Feedback and corrections

    It is important that when the user interface is “done” it its submitted to a limited audience in which potential feedback is given by this audience in order to improve the interface to a better one.

Make a basic business proposal for your solution.

We are looking forward to develop an app and web page in order to improve the stretcher request and transportation.

How we’ll do it?

Well the idea is the following: through an application for mobile devices such as Smartphones or Smart-Watches a doctor can request a stretcher of its convenience and it will arrive as soon as possible without further delay, making the stretcher transportation through the hospital very efficient.

What is needed to develop this project?

  • A full research of the healthcare emergency services.
  • A web page to give promotion and information about the project.
  • An application capable of monitoring the amount and type of stretchers in the hospitals, requesting stretchers, and also estimating its arriving time to where they are needed.
  • Tracking devices for the stretchers

How much money is needed in order to develop the project?

Around $15,000 USD because it is needed to buy all the tracker devices and also to make the connect to the app, the user, and the web page, not to mention the research on healthcare emergency services.

Time needed for develop

It would take around two months for the tracking devices to arrive and then develop the web page, the mobile apps (iOS, Android, including smartwatches too).

¿Cuáles son los requerimientos mínimos que debe tener el sistema para funcionar?

Ya que va a ser una aplicación lo único que deben tener los usuarios es un smartphone, e internet (no requiere muchos datos) para poder utilizar la aplicación con todo el potencial.

Investiga las maneras que puedes usar el iWatch y elige la mejor para tu aplicación.

La forma en que nosotros podríamos utilizar el iWatch es de varias maneras:

La primera es utilizando la pantalla táctil o la corona digital y hacer que los usuario le piquen a lo que desean hacer como en nuestra aplicación de celular.Este método es el más sencillo para los usuarios porque como la mayoria ya sabe cómo navegar las cosas en un celular utilizando la pantalla táctil seria casi lo mismo en el iWatch pero el tamaño sería mucho menor por eso este método es nuestra primer opción.

La segunda manera es por medio de voz, que el usuario diga algunos comandos y que el iWatch haga algo en nuestra aplicación para poder pedir una camilla. Esta manera también sería muy buena porque el usuario solo tendría que decir algunas palabras para poder hacer lo que quiere y sería la manera más eficiente por esto es nuestra segunda opción.

Acorde a tus metas desarrolla los pasos para logra una de ellas. Haz un prototipo para el iWatch.

Un objetivo es la recolección de datos para que la aplicación funcione.

Los pasos a seguir para conseguirlo son:

  • Saber la cantidad de camillas disponibles.(Los camilleros debe saber esta información y ponerlo una vez en la aplicación.)
  • Checar si alguna está siendo utilizada y saber cuántas están disponibles. (Los camilleros en el momento que reciben un notificación para enviar una camilla, deben llevarla y poner en la aplicación que una camilla no está disponible y restarla al total de camillas disponibles.)
  • Poner todos esos datos en la aplicación y mostrarles a los doctores en otra parte de la aplicación. (Va a ver un apps dividida en dos, una es lo que van a utilizar los doctores para pedir camillas y la otra es la que van a utilizar los camilleros para poner la información y que esté disponible para todos.)
¿De qué manera vas a evaluar tu prototipo?

La manera en que nosotros vamos a revisar si nuestro prototipo funciona o es de la mejor manera es recibiendo retroalimentación de los usuarios, porque siempre habrá cosas que nosotros no nos demos cuenta en el momento de hacer la aplicación y que los usuarios piensen que es necesario por eso en recibir retroalimentación se nos hace la mejor manera.
